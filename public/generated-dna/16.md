## 4. Visual Identity Guidelines

### Creative & Visual Direction
A futuristic, bold visual identity with deep, rich colors like charcoal black, electric blue, and neon accents to convey innovation and energy. Typography should be clean and modern, with sans-serif fonts for headlines and a geometric style for body text. Use dynamic gradients, abstract shapes, and lens flare effects to evoke a 'visionary creative laboratory' feel. Photography should feature high-contrast, stylized shots of collaborative teams, data visualizations, and abstract light patterns.

### What NOT to Say / Avoid
