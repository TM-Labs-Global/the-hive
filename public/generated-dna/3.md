## 4. Visual Identity Guidelines

### Creative & Visual Direction
A vibrant, modern aesthetic with a global feel. Use dynamic gradients, world map motifs, and icons representing travel and connectivity. Primary colors could include teal (trust, globality) and coral (adventure, warmth). Clean sans-serif typography for readability and a tech-forward look. Photography should feature diverse travelers engaging in local experiences.
