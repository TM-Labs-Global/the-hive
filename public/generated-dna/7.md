## 4. Visual Identity Guidelines

### Creative & Visual Direction
The visual identity should be modern and clean, with a vibrant color palette that reflects global travel—blues, greens, and golds to evoke trust, growth, and premium quality. Typography should be sleek and sans-serif for readability. Incorporate iconography representing connectivity (eSIM), finance (cards), and local discovery (compass or pin). Use dynamic imagery of diverse destinations and travelers to reinforce the global mindset.

---

## 5. Editorial Guardrails

### What NOT to Say / Avoid
- Avoid overly technical jargon about fintech or telecom
- Avoid discouraging language about foreign exchange or roaming fees in a negative tone
- Avoid promising unrealistic travel experiences
- Avoid inclusive but fake cultural references
- Avoid phrases that downplay the need for local integration
