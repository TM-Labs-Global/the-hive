## 4. Visual Identity Guidelines

### Creative & Visual Direction
A modern, clean design with a palette of deep blues and vibrant accent colors like teal or gold to evoke trust and innovation. Typography should be sans-serif, bold for headlines, and legible for body text. Use imagery of diverse travelers in authentic local settings, with minimalistic icons for cards, eSIM, and location services.
