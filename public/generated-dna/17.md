## 4. Visual Identity Guidelines

### Creative & Visual Direction
A clean, modern design with a vibrant color palette inspired by global travel destinations—think deep blues and warm oranges for contrast. Typography should be sans-serif, bold for headlines, and legible for body text. Imagery should feature diverse, real-world travel scenes highlighting local experiences and effortless digital interactions.

## 5. Editorial Guardrails

### What NOT to Say / Avoid
- Avoid jargon-heavy financial terms
- Avoid over-promising unrealistic savings
- Avoid insensitive or stereotypical depictions of cultures
- Avoid negative comparisons to other brands
- Avoid complex legal disclaimers without context
